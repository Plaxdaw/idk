Our Profile

Mahakam Coffee is a trusted supplier of premium Indonesian coffee beans, dedicated to connecting local farmers with coffee lovers across Indonesia.

Vision

To promote Indonesias rich coffee heritage by delivering authentic, sustainably sourced coffee to every cup.

Mission

  • Support local coffee farmers through fair-trade practices and training.
  • Ensure consistent quality through meticulous bean selection and roasting.
  • Build a strong domestic coffee culture that values Indonesian origin coffee.

Products & Services

  • Green beans & roasted coffee (Arabica & Robusta varieties)
  • Custom roasting & packaging for cafes and retailers
  • Wholesale distribution to local businesses and coffee enthusiasts

Core Values

Quality • Sustainability • Fair Partnership • Indonesian Pride
